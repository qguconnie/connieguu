Film: The Stranger

The film portrays the life of a teenage girl infatuated with social media. She creates an ideal image of herself through posts, exposing her entire life online. The title represents both the stranger who stalks her and the girl herself who digitally fabricates a life she dreams of. Inspired by Ollie Ritchie and Will Darbyshire’s use of music in storytelling, I challenged the traditional way of filmmaking. I placed emphasis on creating the atmosphere, manipulating pauses and rhythms, and making meaning out of them to accompany the cuts of the film.
